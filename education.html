I started my early education when I was 5 years old at Tadika Johan Ceria, Beranang, Selangor. Then, when I was 6 years old, I studied at pre-school Bandar Tasik Kesuma for 1 year. Then, my secondary school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Bandar Tasik Kesuma. I was a school prefect for 3 years. Then, I continued high school at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bandar Tasik Kesuma for 3 years. When I was in form 4, I got offered to the Technical Institute of Kuala Lumpur in electrical engineering studies for 2 years.
